Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Who doesn't love Pepperoni Pizza? Now with this recipe, you can make Pizza at home whenever you want!

Not only does it come together pretty quick and easily, but the end product will be even better than most Pizzas that you can buy!

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces pizza dough, store-bought or homemade
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 18 to 20 slices pepperoni
  • 12 ounces mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano, optional
  • Flour for reolling and shaping dough

    • For the Quick Pizza Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce (no salt)
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon granulated onion
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • teaspoon olive oil

    • For the Honey Butter

  • 1/4 cup softened butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven

    2. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees F.

  2. Make the sauce

    3. If you are using my sauce recipe, stir together the ingredients. The sauce recipe makes just enough for one large pizza. You can easily double it if you are making more than one pizza.

  3. Roll out the dough

    4. Roll out dough on a lightly floured surface. If it's hard to roll, let it rest for minutes so it can come to room temperature. For a large pizza, I like to roll my dough into about a 13-inch diameter circle.

  4. Add the Toppings

    5. Transfer the dough to a lightly dusted pizza peel. Aternatively, fit it into a large cast-iron

    Add sauce in a light layer all over the pizza, leaving about 1/4-inch crust around the edges. Chop half of the pepperoni and spinkle it over the sauce. Top the pizza with grated cheese and the rest of the pepperoni. Season with black pepper.

  5. Cook the Pizza

    6. Press the dough into a cast iron skillet and add toppings. Place the skillet over a high heat burner for 2 minutes to get it preheated and get hte crust cooking right away. Then tranfer to a 500 degree F oven and bake for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.

  6. Slice and server

    7. Use pizza peel to slide pizza out onto a cutting board. Let the pizza rest for a minute and slice into pieces. Season with fresh oregano (optiional). Serve while warm with a side salad.

  7. Make the honey butter (optional)

    8. Stir together the softened butter and honey, then serve alongside the pizza for crust dipping.
