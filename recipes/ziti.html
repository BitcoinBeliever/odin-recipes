Baked Ziti

Description

Baked ziti is classic Italian-American comfort food made with pasta, sausage, tomato sauce , and lots of cheese! Serve this for dinner and I can guarantee it will be a new family hit!

Just be careful not to eat it too often...While it may be tempting, eating this multiple time a week is not advisable, due to the high calorie and fat content.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb dry ziti pasta
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 2 (26 ounce) jars spaghetti sauce
  • 6 ounces Provolone cheese, sliced
  • 1 1/2 cups Sour Cream
  • 6 ounces Mozarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the Pasta

    2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add ziti pasta, and cook until al denta, about 8 minutes; drain.

  2. Cook the Meat & Onion

    3. Meanwhile, brown ground beef and onion in a large skillet over medium heat; stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.

  3. Preheat Oven & prepare baking dish

    4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Butter a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  4. Begin layering

    5. Spread 1/2 of the ziti in the bottom of the prepared dish; top with Provolone cheese, sour cream, 1/2 of the meat sauce, remaining ziti, mozzarella cheese, and remaining meat sauce. Top with grated Parmesan cheese.

  5. Bake

    6. Bake in the prehated oven until heated through and cheeses have melted, about 30 minutes.
